# Handoff Checklist — PCI Compliance Adapter

- [ ] CDE boundary attestation is present and reflects the actual payment scope touched by the change.
- [ ] PAN/CVV redaction rules were applied and no cardholder data remains in the rendered bundle.
- [ ] Operator identity and approver identity are recorded in the bundle metadata.
- [ ] Evidence artifacts resolve to customer-scoped locations only.
- [ ] The final evidence bundle signature was generated with the configured signing backend.
